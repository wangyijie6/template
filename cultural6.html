Anticipated Effects

These ads which are leading kids to a positive life would result in a positive reflection that young generations are likely to imitate what those stars told them to do in the ads. For example, they may go to gym and do homework because these stars told them to do that in media. In these ways, the impact of celebrity branding can be made full use of to lead citizens and young generations to a good direction as well as the whole society.
