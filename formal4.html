Personal Ideas About Choosing Style After the Comparison

By comparing these two essays from different sources, we can easily find out that essays talk about the same field can be really dissimilar in writing just because of the different usages. Thus, choose the correct style to write should be considered as an essential step before starting to write.

I am willing to share my ideas about how to opt the suitable style when write an essay about a specific discipline, here, stat. In my perspective, which style should be chosen really depends on what kind of article you are going to write. In other words, you should know what kind of publication your essay will be on, who your audiences will be, and what situation the essay will be read. To be more specific, if I am asked to write an essay which will be used for academic discussion, or will be used for formal situation, I will not hesitate using the scholarly style. However, if I am going to write an essay for a magazine, I will use more casual words which will not look that serious and are easy for audiences to understand.
