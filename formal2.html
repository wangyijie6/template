Scholarly Essay Analysis

I am now starting with the scholarly essay. After reading it, it is not hard to find out that, as a scholarly essay, the style is indeed formal and professional. Those statements are concise, almost no useless describing sentences. Meanwhile, the essay is apparently written for those who are familiar with this field such like stats experts and researchers.
